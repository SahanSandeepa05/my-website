PLACES

Explore Around Us Rathnapura

  • Bopath Falls

    Bopath Falls is located in the village of Agalwatta in the Ratnapura District of Sri Lanka. The Kuru River, a tributary of the Black River, cascades down 30 meters (95 feet) high through a narrow opening from the top of a mountain at one point in her journey.

  • Sri sumana Saman Devalaya

    Sri sumana Saman is a deity, The name Saman means "the rising morning sun". His character is of historical significance for the Sinhalese people and veneration especially to all the Buddhists.

  • Samanalawewa Reservoir

    Samanalawewa Reservoir is situated close to Belihuloya and it can be accessed via the road that runs from Pambahinna junction. It is a reservoir built to generate hydro power.

  • Kiribathgala mountain

    Still a clean wonderful place. nice view. can cover the full range in a single day if go early morning. also good for camping.

  • Madanagiri Ella(Mohini Ella)

    Mohini Ella- Mohini Water Fall is on Ratnapura Wewelwaththa road about 24km from Ratnapura. It is a very big waterfall. This river helps to make current there is a mini power station. There are big trees on the riverside.

  • Bakers Bend

    Beautiful place which marked as private property and you need to obtain the permission from plantation group to reach here. To see the real beauty and make it memorable you needs to trail it by foot.
