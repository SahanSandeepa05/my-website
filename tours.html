PLACES

Explore Around Us Rathnapura

  • Sri Pada (Adam's Peak)

    Sri Pada, also known as Adam's Peak, is a sacred mountain in Sri Lanka. Pilgrims of all faiths climb it to honor the legendary footprint at the summit, symbolizing spiritual significance.

  • Baker's Bend

    Baker's Bend is a scenic hairpin turn on the Balangoda–Nuwara Eliya road. Surrounded by misty hills and lush forests, it offers breathtaking panoramic views, making it a favorite photo stop for travelers.

  • Wangedigala

    Wangedigala is a popular hiking destination in Sri Lanka, known for its rugged beauty and panoramic views. The trail offers stunning scenery, misty peaks, and a peaceful escape into unspoiled mountain wilderness.

  • Surathali Ella

    Surathali Ella is a beautiful waterfall located in Sri Lanka’s Ratnapura District. Surrounded by lush greenery, its graceful cascade and tranquil setting make it a favorite spot for nature lovers and photographers.

  • Great World's End Drop

    Great World's End Drop is a dramatic cliff in Horton Plains, Sri Lanka, plunging nearly 870 meters. It offers breathtaking views over misty valleys, tea plantations, and distant landscapes—an unforgettable experience for hikers.

  • Devil's Staircase

    Devil's Staircase is a thrilling, zigzag mountain trail in Sri Lanka, known for its sharp bends and steep climbs. Surrounded by misty hills and waterfalls, it's perfect for adventure seekers and nature lovers.
